Our Story

A Family-Run Hotel Built on Good Hospitality

Hotel Green Land started as a small family guest house in Binayi Tribeni Ward No. 06 with one simple belief: every guest should feel at home. Over the years we have grown into a full hotel with an in-house restaurant, but that belief hasn't changed.

Whether you are travelling for work, visiting family, or exploring the natural beauty around Tribeni, our doors — and our kitchen — are always open for you.

  • Family-owned and personally managed
  • Clean rooms at honest, transparent prices
  • Home-style cooking from our own kitchen
  • Trusted by thousands of guests over 10+ years
